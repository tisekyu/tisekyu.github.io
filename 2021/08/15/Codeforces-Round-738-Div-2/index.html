Codeforces Round 738 Div. 2

Posted on 2021-08-15 In competitive programming , codeforces , div2 Disqus:

Round summary

Here's my results (crossing fingers for no FST):

I did Codeforces Round 738 Div. 2 today. I was happy with my speed on A, B, C. Then D2 happened :P. I spent way too long trying to solve the \(n = 10^5\) version, before finally doing the D1 subtask, which was straight implementation.

Then I skipped to E, where I got an immediate solution using NTT, and realized it could be optimized to linear time since we only muliply and divide by linear polynomials by using the geometric series sum. Unfortunately, I decided to time the runtime locally, and I found that it took 5 seconds. I proceeded to spend the next 30 minutes trying to cut down time, and not succeeding, before deciding just to submit and see how bad the solution was. Then it passed in 1.5 seconds :/. Lesson learned, CF is much faster than I expect.

Finally, I return to D2. After doing D1, D2's solution is pretty obvious with a small-to-large merging method. I implement it, and of course fail to implement it correctly for the remainder of the contest, getting RTE on pretest 12 and eventually getting WA on pretest 12. I think my idea is right, but I need to work on implementation.

What I could improve on

Obviously my time management could have been better, and I definitely need to keep in mind that my old ass computer can't be compared with the shiny CF judge. Besides that, not much to say but keep grinding.

Unrelated aside, Mocha's a really awesome name. Only Mocha I know of is from the anime Gochiusa. Was a sweet anime, I highly recommend people watch it to be soothed :D.
